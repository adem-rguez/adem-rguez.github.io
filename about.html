About
Me

Unity & C# Developer building interactive experiences, simulations and real-time systems.

I'm a Unity developer with strong experience in building games, simulations and interactive applications, ranging from VR medical training and enterprise tools to indie games and WebAR experiences.

I've worked on projects for large clients such as Octapharma and Saint-Gobain, delivering production-ready applications used in real professional contexts. Over the years, I've contributed to the successful release of 21 Unity applications on the Google Play Store, and collaborated with multidisciplinary teams on complex interactive systems.
